Mortal Kombat

How to Play Mortal Kombat

Fight your way through legendary warriors in this classic fighting game. Master special moves and combos to defeat your opponents!

  • Arrow Keys: Move character and jump
  • A, S, D: Light, Medium, and Heavy Punch
  • Z, X, C: Light, Medium, and Heavy Kick
  • Combine moves for special attacks and fatalities

Pro Tips

  • Learn your character's special moves
  • Block to avoid taking damage
  • Practice combo chains
  • Time your attacks wisely
