How to Play Papa's Pizzeria

Run your own pizza shop! Take customer orders, prepare pizzas with the right toppings, bake them to perfection, and serve them to earn tips and upgrade your restaurant.

  • Take orders carefully - customers will specify their topping preferences
  • Add toppings in the right amounts and positions as requested
  • Watch the baking time and temperature - don't burn the pizza!
  • Cut the pizza into equal slices as specified
  • Serve quickly to earn better tips and keep customers happy

Remember: The better you follow customer orders, the higher your score and tips will be. Time management is key to success!
