How to Play Learn to Fly

Help a determined penguin achieve his dream of flying! Upgrade your equipment, perfect your technique, and soar through the sky. Earn money from your flights to buy better gear and reach new heights!

  • Space/Up Arrow: Activate rocket boosters
  • Left/Right Arrows: Adjust angle during flight
  • Earn money to buy upgrades between attempts
  • Experiment with different combinations of equipment
  • Try to achieve greater distances and heights with each flight
