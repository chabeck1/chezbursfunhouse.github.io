How to Play Bloons TD 2

Place towers strategically to pop all the balloons before they reach the end of the path. Earn money by popping balloons and upgrade your towers to handle stronger waves!

  • Click to place towers
  • Upgrade towers by clicking on them
  • Different towers are effective against different types of balloons
  • Watch out for special balloon types!
