Cubefield

How to Play Cubefield

Navigate through an endless field of cubes at high speed. Test your reflexes and see how far you can go without crashing! The game gets progressively faster and more challenging as you advance.

  • Left/Right Arrow Keys: Steer your ship
  • Space: Pause game
  • P: Toggle between normal and hard mode
  • Try to survive as long as possible and beat your high score!
